The "5 Whys" Linkage

1. Why is the result missing? → Skill gap identified.
2. Why that gap? → Misunderstood concept in video.
3. Why misunderstood? → Cognitive overload.
4. Why overload? → No breakdown of steps.
5. THE ROOT: Inefficient mental modeling.
